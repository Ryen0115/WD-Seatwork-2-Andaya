CAPTAIN PRICE

“This is for the record — history is written by the victor.”

Background

Captain John Price is a legendary British SAS operator known for his sharp tactics, gritty leadership, and unwavering loyalty. From covert raids to high-risk extraction missions, he stands at the forefront of the most classified military operations in the world. Calm under pressure and lethal on the battlefield, Price is the backbone of Task Force 141.

  • Secondary: .50 GS Desert Eagle
  • Equipment: Flash Grenade, Thermite
  • Perks: Hardline, Quick Fix, Ghost
