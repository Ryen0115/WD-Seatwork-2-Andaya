Ghost

"We get dirty, and the world stays clean."

Background Story

Ghost, real name Simon Riley, is a legendary British special forces operator known for his iconic skull mask, tactical expertise, and psychological warfare. Operating in the shadows, Ghost is a master of infiltration, assassination, and staying off the radar. Haunted by a tragic past and driven by vengeance, his presence strikes fear even before a bullet is fired. Silent. Ruthless. Unstoppable.

Loadout

  • Primary: M4 – Tactical Suppressor, Red Dot Sight
  • Secondary: 1911 Pistol – Fast Reload
  • Lethal: Semtex
  • Tactical: Flashbang
  • Perks: Ghost, Dead Silence, Quick Fix
